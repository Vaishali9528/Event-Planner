Top Weddings Destination In India



1. Udaipur,Rajasthan


Of all destinations in India that are known to be perfect for weddings, Udaipur always comes out on top and for good reason. Its clear blue skies, glorious palaces and breathtaking lakes make the most splendid setting for a regal ceremony. Rest assured, your wedding pictures will turn out to be super colourful and vibrant! An advantage is your guests will have a lot to do once the festivities are over, from going on sightseeing tours to indulging in exciting activities like boating on Pichola Lake.

2. Jaipur,Rajasthan


With its age-old palaces, magnificent forts and grand mansions, Jaipur seems absolutely perfect for the most luxurious wedding celebrations. It gives you a chance to get married like kings and queens at top-notch venues that aren’t anything brief of picture-perfect backdrops. Adding to the grandeur of the ceremonies would be the local pagdi-walas, mehndi artists, folk dancers, sarangi players and more. A plus is that your guests can reach Jaipur in a breeze by air and train and even by car.

3. Kerala


Sounds like an offbeat choice, right? But you’d be surprised how the quiet and picturesque setting in Kerala can set just the right tone for your union. Whether you choose to tie the knot amidst the sun-kissed beaches of Kovalam or the pristine backwaters of Alleppey, you’ll find myriad resorts that will leave your guests mesmerized in a moment. Add a touch of Kerala’s rich culture to your festivities by serving South Indian fare on banana leaves and organising a party on a traditional houseboat.

4. Andaman and Nicobar Islands


Wish to say ‘I do’ far away from the mainland amidst azure-coloured skies, clear waters and golden sands? Think of Andaman and Nicobar Islands. While most parts of it are not accessible to tourists, the Havelock Island has many seaside resorts for you to get married at. Did you know you can even book an entire island resort and exchange vows in the backdrop of the Indian Ocean? The pleasing weather of Andaman and Nicobar Islands is all the more reason for you to get married here.

5. Goa


When talking about getting married by the sea, there’s no way you can’t think about marrying your special someone in Goa. It is such a mixed bag of pristine beaches, breathtaking sunsets, historical churches, beautiful resorts and verdant forests that you can have a variety of pictures for your pre-wedding shoot. You can also revel in Goa’s party vibe by organising the haldi and mehndi festivities by the pool, rain dances in the middle of the sea or an endless night of cocktails on the beach.

6. Mussoorie, Uttarakhand


Dreaming of a beautiful wedding ceremony amidst the mountains? Well, Mussoorie surely has the right postcard backdrop for a dreamy wedding. Beautiful tree-covered hills, the surreal view of the sun setting against the Himalayas, the clear skies, the cool breeze and the twinkling stars at night – everything about Mussoorie has a wonderful romantic air to it. You can even arrive before your D-Day and get some heavenly pictures clicked in the pristine surroundings of the hill station.

7. Gurugram-Manesar,Haryana


If you’re looking to not get too far from your home to get married yet have a destination wedding experience, then you’d agree! There is a wide variety of resorts and hotels in Gurgaon and Manesar that will take care of all your wedding planning needs and provide you just the best theme setup for your Big Day – from royal to minimalistic. If you’re from Delhi or neighbouring cities, this might be for you!

8. Jim Corbett, Uttarakhand


If you have always dreamt of a destination wedding but at the same time you don’t wish to splurge, then Jim Corbett is just the perfect place for you. Fast emerging as a popular affordable destination for weddings in India, this tree-laden land will provide you and your partner the perfect opportunity to celebrate one of the biggest days of your life away from the hustle and bustle of the city. Imagine taking pheras under a beautiful mandap surrounded by forested hills amidst chilly mountain air!

9. Jaisalmer,Rajasthan


Guests are in for a magical and mesmerizing treat and experience at a wedding in a desert land. The ultimate cultural immersion makes guests happier in every sense. Apart from the rituals soaked in Indian traditions like haldi, sangeet,mehndi, guests can enjoy added activities and adventures like camel, elephant riding, puppet show, non-stop music, folk songs and impromptu dance performances.

10. Himachal Pradesh


Shimla is one of the popular summer wedding destinations in India which offers a peaceful environment, quiet atmosphere, nice climate, and snow-covered peaks , pleasant climate and snow covered hills.
